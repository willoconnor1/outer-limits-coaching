What to Expect

Are you experiencing feelings of "what now…what's next" with no clear answer?

What gets me excited? Seeing people step into their fullest potential—whether they're changing careers, navigating big life transitions, or simply ready to break free from the ordinary.

After years of mentoring teams, I've discovered one thing: the power to transform is already within you. Sometimes, you just need the right opportunity and space to help unlock it.

At Outer Limits Coaching we will focus on the following:

Goal Setting

Define clear, specific, and achievable goals.

Self-Awareness

Explore thoughts, feelings, and behaviors to help you better understand your strengths and areas for improvement, leading to personal growth.

Overcoming Obstacles

Identify potential challenges and barriers that may hinder your progress and provide strategies and support to navigate these obstacles effectively.

Action Planning

Create structured plans including identifying resources, setting timelines in order to achieve your goals.

Accountability Partner

As progress is made towards reaching your goals, I will support and be there to help you stay on track!
