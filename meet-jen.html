Meet Jen

It all begins with an idea.

I knew early on that I wanted to learn more about what makes people tick, so I completed my Undergrad Degree in Sociology, a Post Grad Diploma in Human Resource Management and am a Certified Professional Coach.

Coaching has always come naturally to me, starting as a camp counselor and director, followed by 13 years as a recruiter focused on helping people create and realize their ideal career.

I spent the next 14 years hiring and mentoring global teams within sales organizations in startups and corporate America at companies like NetSuite, Oracle and Tipalti. During my career, I experienced my fair share of highs and lows, and benefited from mentors who encouraged me to advocate for myself and go for what I wanted.

If you're asking yourself, What Now? What's Next?...I would love the opportunity to chat and learn more about where you're currently at and where you want to go!
